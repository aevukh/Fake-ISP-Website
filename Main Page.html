How we do what we do

  • We are able to accomplish all that we do thanks to your continued support, and we are extraordinarily grateful for that.

Technologically speaking, for our free wifi, we make it so that the devices you use to connect to our wifi transmit their own wifi signals to those surrounding you (this does not mean that those around you can see your internet history, nor what you browse). In addition to this, every hookup we have on telephone poles broadcasts their own wifi signal, and they all have the same ip, so you don't have to keep reconnecting to the wifi.
